About Us

Digital Shovel is a rapidly growing tech manufacturing start-up based in Scarborough. Celebrating our 5th year in operation, we are proud to be one of the leaders in both scale and innovation within the blockchain infrastructure space in Canada.

Our 70,000 sq ft manufacturing facility enables us to build modular data centers and develop new infrastructure related to the industry on site from the ground up. Our success stems from a dedicated and talented team of individuals who are passionate about pushing the boundaries of technology.

Scot Johnson

CEO & President

Scot Johnson is the CEO and President of Digital Shovel. He has over 10 years of experience as a business owner, over 5 years in the technology sector and is passionate about innovation and efficiency in the crypto industry.

Persia Khodarahmi

Operations Manager

Persia Khodarahmi is the Operations Manager at Digital Shovel. She is responsible for overseeing the day-to-day operations and is passionate about delivering high-quality products and services to clients and fostering a culture of excellence and collaboration.

Manesha Persaud

Manesha Persaud has over 4 years of driving company results with talent. She is truly passionate about attracting and retaining top talent to help the company thrive. She is dedicated to ensuring that our company consistently acquires the best professionals in the industry.
